My Favourite Websites

Catogries of Website

1. Learning & Education: Khan Academy

A nonprofit platform offering thousands of free video lessons and practice exercises across subjects like math, science, history, literature, and more. It’s a go-to for students of all ages looking to learn independently or supplement school learning

Khan Academy

2. Documentation & Development: MDN Web Docs

The gold standard for web technology documentation—covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Web APIs, and beyond. Maintained by Mozilla and community contributors, it’s perfect for developers needing authoritative and up-to-date web standards reference.

MDN Web Docs

3. Education for Kids: BBC Bitesize

Packed with curriculum-aligned videos, interactive activities, and quizzes, BBC Bitesize offers engaging learning content for school students. Particularly strong for UK curriculum support, but widely valuable worldwide for its quality & variety.

BBC Bitesize

4. Fun Learning: Starfall

A delightful early-childhood education site—Starfall uses interactive phonics-based games and stories to help kids learn reading fundamentals (ABCs, phonemes, basic words). Free to use, ad-free, and research-based.

Starfall

5. Entertainment & Inspiration: Cool Math Games

This site delivers logic- and strategy-based games that are both entertaining and educational. With a focus on non-violent, brain-stimulating content, it's a popular place for young minds to have fun while thinking critically.

Cool Math Games
