RECIPE FOR DOSA

INGREDIENTS:

  • For Batter
    • Rice -> 2 cups
    • Urad dal -> 1 cup
    • Fenugreek seeds -> 1 tsp
    • Salt -> to taste
  • For Cooking
    • Oil or ghee -> as needed

STEPS:

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6-8 hours.
  2. Grind into smooth batter and add salt.
  3. Leave batter to ferment overnight.
  4. Heat a pan and pour a ladle of batter, spreading thinly.
  5. Drizzle oil/ghee around edges and cook until golden brown.
  6. Fold dosa and serve hot with chutney or sambar.

TIPS

  1. Use a non-stick pan for easy flipping.
  2. Fermentation is key for crispiness.
  3. Serve immediately for best taste.
