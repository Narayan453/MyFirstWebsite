RECIPE FOR NOODLES

INGREDIENTS:

  • Noodles -> 200g
  • Vegetables -> 1 cup (carrots, capsicum, beans)
  • Soy sauce -> 2 tbsp
  • Oil -> 1 tbsp
  • Garlic -> 2 cloves

STEPS:

  1. Boil noodles in water until soft and drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add cooked noodles and soy sauce, toss well.
  5. Cook for another 2 minutes and serve hot.

TIPS

  1. Add a pinch of black pepper for extra flavor.
  2. Use fresh vegetables for crunchiness.
  3. Serve immediately to keep noodles soft and tasty.
