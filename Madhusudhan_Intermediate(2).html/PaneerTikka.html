RECIPE FOR PANEER TIKKA

INGREDIENTS:

  • Paneer -> 200g (cut into cubes)
  • Yogurt -> 2 tbsp
  • Spices -> 1 tsp each (turmeric, red chili, garam masala)
  • Lemon juice -> 1 tsp
  • Oil -> 1 tbsp
  • Bell peppers & onions -> optional

STEPS:

  1. Mix yogurt, spices, and lemon juice to make marinade.
  2. Coat paneer and vegetables in the marinade.
  3. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Skewer paneer and veggies and grill or pan-fry until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney.

TIPS

  1. Do not overcrowd the pan while cooking.
  2. Marinate well for richer taste.
  3. Use fresh paneer for soft texture.
