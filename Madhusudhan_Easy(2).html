Hobbies

  • Cricket

    Cricket is a popular sport played between two teams of eleven players, where one team bats to score runs and the other bowls and fields to restrict them. The game is played on a pitch with a bat and ball, and runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between wickets or hitting boundaries. It is known for formats like Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20), each offering a unique style of play.

  • Football

    Football, also known as soccer, is the world’s most popular sport played between two teams of eleven players. The main objective is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent’s net while following simple rules like not using hands, except by the goalkeeper. Played on a rectangular field with a goal at each end, football is fast-paced, exciting, and celebrated globally through leagues, tournaments, and the FIFA World Cup.

  • Esports

    Esports, short for electronic sports, is competitive video gaming where players or teams compete in popular games like League of Legends, Dota 2, and Counter-Strike. It has grown into a global phenomenon with professional tournaments, huge prize pools, and millions of online viewers. Esports combines skill, strategy, and teamwork, making it one of the fastest-growing forms of entertainment and competition worldwide.
